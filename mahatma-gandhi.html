Here's the timeline of Gandhi ji

  • 1869 - Born in Porbandar.

  • 1888 - Sailed from Bombay to London to pursue law.

  • 1891 - Returned to India.

  • 1893-1914 - Civil Rights activist in South Africa.

  • 1915-1947 - Struggle for Indian-Independence.

  • 1920 - Started Non-Cooperation Movement

  • 1931-1932 - Round Table Conference

  • 1942 - Quit India Movement

  • 1948 - Assassination by Nath Ram Godse
